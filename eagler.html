About Eaglercraft

Minecraft 1.8.8 that runs entirely in your browser. No downloads, no plugins — just pure HTML5 and WebGL.

Controls:
WASD / Arrow Keys to move · Space to jump · E to open inventory · Left click to mine/attack · Right click to place/use

Features:
• Singleplayer worlds (saves in browser)
• Multiplayer support (join servers)
• Works on Chromebooks, Windows, Mac, Linux

Eaglercraft is not affiliated with Mojang · Repositories load live from GitHub API
